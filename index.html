Interesting facts about roses

  • The earliest roses have been traced back to some 35 million years ago
  • Cleopatra’s living quarters were filled with rose petals to remind Marc Antony of her every time he smelt roses
  • Roses have been referenced in ancient Buddhist and Confucian religious documents

Interesting facts about lions

  1. A good gauge of a male lion’s age is the darkness of his mane. The darker the mane, the older the lion.
  2. A lion’s heels don’t touch the ground when it walks.
  3. A lion may sleep up to 20 hours a day.

ORigami owl

Interesting facts about owls

  1. The eyes of an owl are not true “eyeballs.” Their tube-shaped eyes are completely immobile, providing binocular vision which fully focuses on their prey and boosts depth perception.
  2. A group of owls is called a parliament. This originates from C.S. Lewis’ description of a meeting of owls in The Chronicles of Narnia.
  3. The Northern Hawk Owl can detect—primarily by sight—a vole to eat up to a half a mile away.
